# Recommendations

## Target: Personal Laptop
## Based On: Findings from Security Audit

---

### 🔴 High Priority (Fix Immediately)

**1. Update the Operating System**
- Go to your system settings and install all pending updates.
- Updates patch known security vulnerabilities that attackers actively exploit.
- *Windows:* Settings → Windows Update → Check for Updates
- *Mac:* System Settings → General → Software Update

**2. Secure Browser Saved Passwords**
- Stop relying on the browser alone to store passwords.
- Use a dedicated password manager such as **Bitwarden** (free) or **1Password**.
- A password manager encrypts your passwords and protects them with one strong master password.

---

### 🟡 Medium Priority (Fix This Week)

**3. Enable Automatic Updates**
- Turn on automatic updates so the OS and apps stay updated without needing manual action.
- This ensures you are always protected against newly discovered vulnerabilities.

**4. Remove Unused or Suspicious Applications**
- Go through your installed apps and uninstall anything you no longer use.
- Each unused app is an unnecessary security risk.
- *Windows:* Settings → Apps → Installed Apps
- *Mac:* Finder → Applications → Drag unused apps to Trash

**5. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**
- Turn on 2FA for your most important accounts (email, social media, banking).
- Even if your password is stolen, 2FA prevents unauthorized login.
- Use an authenticator app like **Google Authenticator** or **Authy**.

---

### 🟢 Good Practice (Ongoing Habits)

**6. Avoid Installing Apps from Untrusted Sources**
- Only download software from official websites or trusted app stores.
- Avoid cracked software — it often contains malware.

**7. Lock Your Screen When Away**
- Always lock your screen when stepping away from your laptop.
- *Windows:* Press `Windows + L`
- *Mac:* Press `Control + Command + Q`

**8. Back Up Your Data Regularly**
- Use an external drive or cloud backup to protect your data.
- If your device is ever attacked (e.g., ransomware), a backup saves you from losing everything.
